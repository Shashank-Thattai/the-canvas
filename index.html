about

This is a personal site, built feature by feature, day by day. It is not finished. It will not be finished. What you see today is what was added today; what you see tomorrow will be different.

I am building this in the open, in the manner of someone who believes that a site is made by accumulation, not by revelation. Each addition is small. Each is necessary. Each remains.

If you find yourself here, you are welcome to stay.
