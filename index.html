snapshots

Capture the state of this site at any moment by pressing s. Return here to see what has changed since you last looked, and what has remained the same.

How to use: Press s anywhere on the site to save a snapshot of the current page. Each snapshot records the date, time, and visible content. Return to this page to compare snapshots and see what has shifted.

    about

    This is a personal site, built feature by feature, day by day. It is not finished. It will not be finished. What you see today is what was added today; what you see tomorrow will be different.

    I am building this in the open, in the manner of someone who believes that a site is made by accumulation, not by revelation. Each addition is small. Each is necessary. Each remains.

    If you find yourself here, you are welcome to stay.

    reading

    Books and essays I have read, am reading, or intend to read. Each entry carries a note on why it earned its place.

      notes

      A line you are carrying. A question you have not yet placed. A thing you noticed recently and have not yet named. Leave it here. The site will remember.

      log

      A record of what you have left behind. Each line is dated and sealed. Read back through the nights you have walked.
